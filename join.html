 There are many great reasons to join the Ohio State chapter of the ACM-W, and we'd be thrilled to have you in our organization.

Here are just a few of the reasons to join us:
  • To help your community
  • To make new friends who share your interest in technology
  • To have participation in a reputable professional association on your resume
  • To be a top pick for employers
  • To receive emails about exciting job opportunities
  • To meet with potential employers face-to-face
  • To have fun! The ACM-W sponsors quarterly events, such as trips to haunted houses, laser tag, and gaming competitions.
If any of those sound appealing to you, we invite you to join us!

 The ACM-W is open to all students who are interested in technology, regardless of gender, major, or university status.

 To find information about our next meeting, please see the Events Page. Also, to receive the most up-to-date information in your inbox, be sure to join our Mailing List.
